How It Works

Getting your gaming reward on ElevateIQ is simple — just 4 easy steps to claim your favourite reward.

1

🎮 Join Our Discord Server

Start by joining the official ElevateIQ Discord server. This is your hub for earning IQCoins through invites, messages, daily rewards, and much more — all tracked automatically by our bot.

Completely free — takes under 30 seconds
2

🪙 Earn IQCoins

There are multiple ways to stack up your IQCoins balance:

  • 📅 Daily Claim — Claim 1,000 free coins every 24 hours
  • 📺 Watch Ad — Watch a short ad and earn 50,000 coins
  • 💬 50 Messages — Chat in Discord and earn 5,000 coins
  • 👥 5 Invites — Invite friends and earn 10,000 coins
The more you earn, the bigger your reward
3

🎫 Redeem With Code or Coins

Two ways to get your reward:

  • Redeem a Code — Already have an ElevateIQ code? Redeem it directly (Minecraft ₹899, Roblox ₹1,499+, Xbox ₹1,999)
  • Coin Rewards — Have enough IQCoins? Exchange them for a reward at no extra cost
Both options are fully secure and verified
4

✅ Activated Within 72 Hours

After redemption, your reward is activated to your account within 72 hours. Track your order status in real-time on the Track Order page. You can also download your invoice as a PDF for your records.

Average activation time: 12–24 hours

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026 — Your privacy is our top priority. Here's exactly what we collect and why.

1. Information We Collect

When you use ElevateIQ, we may collect the following information:

  • Discord Account Info — When you log in with Discord, we receive your Discord username, User ID, and avatar. We never see or store your Discord password.
  • IQCoins Activity — Your coin balance, earning history, and redemption history.
  • Order Details — When you redeem a reward: game type, selected plan, and invoice information.
  • Usage Data — Basic analytics data such as pages visited and time spent on the site, to help us improve the experience.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Processing and delivering your reward orders
  • Tracking and updating your IQCoins balance
  • Providing customer support
  • Improving the website and fixing bugs
  • Fraud prevention and security

3. Data Sharing

We never sell your personal data to any third party. Data is only shared in the following cases:

  • Discord API — For login verification (with your explicit permission)
  • Microsoft OAuth — For account verification (with your explicit permission)
  • MongoDB Atlas — Secure, encrypted database storage
  • Legal requirements — If required by applicable law or legal process

4. Data Security

Your data is stored securely in MongoDB Atlas using industry-standard encryption. We never store your gaming passwords — only OAuth tokens issued by Discord and Microsoft are used for verification.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to store your session and coin balance locally on your device. This data never leaves your browser unless you explicitly interact with our platform. You can clear it at any time through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to request deletion of your data
  • Right to close your account and remove all associated data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@elevateiq.net.

7. Google AdSense

This website uses Google AdSense to serve advertisements. Google may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to personalize ads based on your browsing activity. You can manage or opt out of personalized ads via Google Ad Settings.

8. Children's Privacy

ElevateIQ is not intended for users under the age of 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this website. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal data, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Significant changes will be announced on our Discord server and on this website. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact: support@elevateiq.net
