🙋 Before You Contact Us

Check these common questions — you might find your answer instantly:

My reward hasn't arrived yet ▼
If it's been over 72 hours, email us at +91 8447927916 with your invoice number and we'll resolve it on priority.
I entered the wrong account details ▼
Contact us immediately on Discord #support or via email with your invoice number. If the reward hasn't been activated yet, we may be able to correct it.
My code is showing as invalid ▼
Make sure to enter your code carefully — hyphens are added automatically. If the issue persists, contact support with that code and we'll investigate.
How do I get a refund? ▼
If your reward wasn't delivered within 72 hours and we're unable to fix it, you'll receive a full refund in IQCoins or payment. Raise a case via email.
Can I change my redeemed account? ▼
Contact us via Discord immediately after redemption. If the reward hasn't been processed yet, we can try to update the account details.
